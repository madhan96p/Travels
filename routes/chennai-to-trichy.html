About the Chennai to Trichy Route

The journey from Chennai to Trichy covers a distance of approximately 330 km and takes around 6 Hours hours via NH-45/NH-44.

Visit the majestic Rockfort Temple and Srirangam, the heart of Tamil Nadu.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the taxi fare from Chennai to Trichy?

Our fares start at ₹5200 for a Sedan and ₹7200 for an Innova. Prices may vary based on date and availability.

Is the driver batta included?

For one-way drops, driver batta is usually included. For round trips, a daily driver allowance (₹300-₹500) applies.

Can I book a one-way drop?

Yes, we specialize in one-way drops to Trichy so you don't pay for the return journey of the car.
