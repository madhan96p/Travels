Frequently Asked Questions

What is the taxi fare from Chennai to Thiruvarur?

Our fares start at ₹4800 for a Sedan and ₹6500 for an Innova. Prices may vary based on date and availability.

Is the driver batta included?

For one-way drops, driver batta is usually included. For round trips, a daily driver allowance (₹300-₹500) applies.

Can I book a one-way drop?

Yes, we specialize in one-way drops to Thiruvarur so you don't pay for the return journey of the car.
