Common Pricing Questions

Time and kilometers are calculated from the point our vehicle leaves our garage (shed) until it returns. This is a standard and fair practice for calculating local hire charges.

All our estimates exclude Toll fees, Parking charges, and State Entry Permits. These variable costs are charged based on actuals at the end of your trip to ensure complete transparency.
