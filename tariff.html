Billing Questions?

Transparent answers. No hidden surprises.

How is the 'Local Package' calculated?

Local packages are for city use (5hrs/50km). If you exceed 5 hours OR 50km, extra charges apply per hour/km as shown in the tariff card.

How does Outstation billing work?

Outstation is billed on a "Per KM" basis with a minimum coverage (250km/day). Driver Batta is charged per calendar day. Tolls & Parking are extra.

Is there a night driving charge?

Yes, night driver allowance is applicable for drives between 10:00 PM and 5:00 AM. This goes directly to the driver.

Do you provide GST Bills?

Yes. We provide GST-compliant invoices for corporate claims. Please mention your GSTIN at the time of booking.
