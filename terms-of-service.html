Our quoted prices typically include fuel and driver charges. However, the following are excluded and must be paid by the customer directly:

2. Kilometer Calculation

For local packages, kilometers and hours are calculated from our garage (Shed) back to our garage (Shed).

Our drivers are instructed to drive within speed limits. Please do not force drivers to overspeed. Driver BATA covers their food and accommodation.

4. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 24 hours prior to the trip will receive a full refund (if advance was paid). Cancellations within 4 hours of the trip may attract a cancellation fee.
